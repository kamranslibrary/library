  • Civil Justice Review (1988)

    • It recommended to move away from “Strict Adversarial Approach to Litigation”
    • It recommended two basic changes:
      • “Party Controlled Case Management” to be replaced by “Court Controlled Case Management”
      • “Cards on the Table Approach” to be adopted
      • Improved Discovery Regime
      • “Substantive Justice” remained the Framework
     Download

  • HeilBrone Hodge Report (1993)

    • It recommended that the framework of “Substantive Justice” was faulty
    • Aim of Civil Justice System should be shifted from “Substantive Justice” to “Settlement”
    • However, “Substantive Justice” remained the Overriding Objective of the Justice System
